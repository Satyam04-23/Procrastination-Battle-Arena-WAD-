About the company

Procrastination Battle Arena is the battle against your procrastination. IT motivates employees, students, and other working professionals to complete their work, assignments, and tasks using the concept of gamification. This is not a normal to-do list or task manager system, which only reminds the user about their pending works and submissions. It connects with the user emotionally, friendly, as a mentor, friend which takes care of their work, saves their time, and increases productivity. This is like a game for which you will be rewarded for the completion of work registered. It's the easiest and playful way to end procrastination.
